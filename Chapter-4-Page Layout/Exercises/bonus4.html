About

The preferred environment for installing and utilizing software is quickly evolving to the web.
Web technologies are essential to how we manage our professional and social lives, work together with like-minded individuals, enjoy art, and find entertainment.Anyone wishing to pursue a career
in computers should possess at least a basic grasp of web development, as well as the ability to
recognize the main advantages and disadvantages that web-based software offers.

Overview

The development of online interactive experiences is the main topic of this subject.
You obtain a practical grasp of how the major web development languages (HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript)
manage content structure/styling and user interaction. We presumptively have little to no prior
web building expertise. You pick up new skills and best practices as you go along by tackling coding problems and
creative briefs that help you build your programming "toolkit."
